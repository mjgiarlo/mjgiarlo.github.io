Projects

Current

  • Penn State Digital Stewardship: an institutional initiative to address digital content and data management needs in areas such as digital library collections, scholarly communications, electronic records archiving, and e-science/e-research data management
  • CURATEcamp: a series of unconference-style events focused on connecting practitioners and technologists interested in digital curation
  • ScholarSphere: a Ruby on Rails application utilizing the Blacklight and Hydra-head gems for integration with the search & indexing system, Solr, and the digital asset management platform, Fedora Commons, serving as Penn State’s institutional repository
  • Hydra: a collaboratively developed suite of repository components
  • active-fedora: a Rails module integrating Fedora Commons as a persistence back-end, a component of the Hydra framework
  • noid: a Ruby library implementing the Nice Opaque Identifier specification
  • arkpy: a basic implemention of Archival Resource Key utils in Python
